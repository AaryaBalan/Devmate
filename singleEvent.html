All that you need to know about Encore Indian (Hindi Solo Singing) About: Discover the magic of 'Encore Indian' â€“ a captivating Hindi solo singing event. Join us for an unforgettable showcase of soulful melodies, timeless classics, and mesmerizing performances. Let the music weave its spell as 'Encore Indian' unfolds the beauty of Indian tunes, creating a memorable experience for all! Team Size: Solo Rounds: Online Prelims, Finals Judging Criteria: Accuracy of notes, Rhythm, Voice Modulation/'Murki'/'Harkat', Emotion and Overall Impact of the performance Online Prelims: Time Limit: 2 minutes The participant should record a video of any song of his/her choice. The chosen song should abide by the competition guidelines. No backing tracks or instruments are allowed for the online round. During registration, participants must submit the Google Drive link to their video. Keep the access "Open To All". Registration Deadline: 28th February, 2025 This round is free for participation. Finals: Time Limit: 5 minutes Date of event: TBA This round will be held live at the organizing institute IIIT Delhi. For backing tracks/instruments, participants are allowed to choose from only one of the following options: The participant may play an instrument him/herself. The participant may use one accompanist. The participant may sing over a karaoke track. More information will be given to the shortlisted participants after the prelims. Finalists shall be required to pay a small commitment fee of Rs 50. Rules: Songs must be in Hindi Songs must not be purely classical (although classical portions are permissible) Songs must not include any rap portions. Participants disobeying the time limit will be penalized. The accompanist must be from the participant's own college (only applicable for the Finals). The decision of the judge(s) shall be final and binding. Note: In case of any disputes, the decision of Odyssey IIITD would be final. For any queries, feel free to contact the organizers!
